I was able to summit Rainbow Peak for the second time on March 22nd, 2025. It was a beautiful ~40 degree early spring day. This peak is usually one of the first snowless peaks of the year and 2025 was no different. I was able to get to the top with minimal potholing. The view from the top is awesome!

<CenteredImage image={map} altText={'Rainbow Ridge 3D Map'} width={750} height={750} />


The way up is pretty easy. There might be some 3rd class terrain on the ridge leading up to the headwall. I wouldn't be worried about taking anyone that is in decent physical shape up this one. They might be a little squeamish in a few spots if they don't like heights. Also, keep in mind that this is a popular spot for tourists. Tourists love kicking rocks down on the people below. Be careful out there!

<CenteredImage image={rainbowRidge2} altText={'Rainbow Ridge'} title={'Rainbow Ridge'} width={500} height={500} />

There is one place where the trail Y's off and you can go right. On the way up I went climber's right and it led to excessive side-hilling and steeper climbing. Stick to the right, it goes!
