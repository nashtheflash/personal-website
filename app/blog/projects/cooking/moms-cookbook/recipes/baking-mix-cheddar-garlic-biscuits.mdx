## Baking Mix Cheddar Garlic Biscuits

### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient              |
| :------------: | :---------------------: |
| 2 Cups         | Baking Mix              |
| 2/3 Cup        | Milk                    |
| 3/4 Cup        | Shredded Cheddar Cheese |
| 2 Tablespoons  | Melted Butter           |
| 1/8 teaspoons  | Garlic Powder           |

### Method
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Mix first 3 ingredients to form soft dough. Drop by 9 spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes. Mix butter and garlic powder; brush over warm biscuits.
