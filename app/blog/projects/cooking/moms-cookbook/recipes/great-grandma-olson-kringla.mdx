## Great Grandma Olson's Kringla
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure   | Ingredient   |
| :--------------: | :----------: |
| 1/2 Cup          | Butter       |
| 1 Cup            | Sugar        |
| 1 Teaspoon       | Vanilla      |
| 1 Large          | Egg          |
| 2 Teaspoons      | Soda         |
| 3 1/2 Cups       | Flour        |
| 3 Teaspoons      | Baking Powder|
| 1 pint           | Sour Cream   |

### Method
Cream butter, sugar, vanilla and egg. Sift dry ingredients and add alternately with sour cream. Chill overnight. Roll as for cutouts, slightly thicker. Cut into 1/2" slices about 9-10  inches long and form into fight eight shapes. Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes.

### Story
Great Grandma Edna Olson's Kringla were a staple of holiday gatherings. They have a subtle and sweet but rather nondescript taste... We like to laugh and ask is it similar to the temperament of a Norwegian herself?\
**-- Nancy**
