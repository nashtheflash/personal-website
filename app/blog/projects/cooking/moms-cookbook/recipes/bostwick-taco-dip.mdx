## Bostwick's Taco Dip
### Ingredients
#### Layer 1 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure   | Ingredient       |
| :--------------: | :--------------: |
| 1x 10-1/2 Ounce  | Jalapeno Bean Dip|
| 1x 15 Ounce Can  | Refried Beans    |
#### Layer 2 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure   | Ingredient              |
| :--------------: | :---------------------: |
| 1x Container     | Avacado dip(not to thin)|
| 1/4-1/2 Cup      | Chunky Salsa            |
#### Layer 3 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure   | Ingredient                 |
| :--------------: | :------------------------: |
| 1 Cup            | Sour Cream                 |
| 1/2 Cup          | Miracle Whip Salad Dressing|
| 1 Package        | Taco Seasoning mix         |
#### Layer 4 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient                    |
| :------------: | :---------------------------: |
| 1 Cup          | Chopped Green Onions          |
| 2 Cup          | Chopped Tomatoes              |
| 1x 8 Ounce Bag | Shredded Cheddar Cheese       |
| 1 Can          | sliced and Pitted Black Olives|
| Side Serving   | Tortill Chips                 |

### Method
Spread each layer, one at a time beginning with the first, onto a cookie sheet. Use chips to scoop.\
