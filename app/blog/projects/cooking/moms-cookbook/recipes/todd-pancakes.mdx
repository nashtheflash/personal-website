## Todds's Pancakes
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient    |
| :------------: | :-----------: |
| 5/8 Cup        | Flour         |
| 1/2 Tablespoon | Baking Powder |
| 1/2 Tablespoon | Sugar         |
| 1/4 Teaspoon   | Salt          |
| 5/8 Cup        | Milk          |
| 1 Large        | Egg           |
| 2 Tablespoons  | Margarine     |

### Method
Beat well ingredients together with wire whisk. Serves 2.

### Additional Information
Todd and I went on a retreat in college while we were dating. His host family served whese pancakes and he requested the recipe. Since then, he has become the Pancake-King to our weekend breakfast delight.
