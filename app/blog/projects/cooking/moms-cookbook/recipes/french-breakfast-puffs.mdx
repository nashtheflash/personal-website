## French Breakfast Puffs
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure  | Ingredient       |
| :-------------: | :--------------: |
| 1/3 Cup         | Butter           |
| 1/2 Cup         | Sugar            |
| 1 Large         | Egg              |
| 1 1/2 Cup       | Flour            |
| 1 1/2 Teaspoons | Baking Powder    |
| 1/2 Teaspoon    | Salt             |
| 1/4 Teaspoon    | Nutmeg, optional |
| 1/2 Cup         | Milk             |
| 3 tablespoons   | Melted Butter    |
| 1/2 Cup         | Sugar            |
| 1 Teaspoon      | Cinnamon         |

### Method
Mix butter, sugar and egg well. Sift dry ingredients and add alternately with milk. Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake 20-25 minutes at 350. Melt 3 tablespoons butter; separately, combine 1/2 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Dip muffin tops in butter and then cinnamon sugar mixture. Serve warm.

### Additional Information
My mom made these many Saturday mornings while I was growing up. They are on of my favorite special breakfast treats.
