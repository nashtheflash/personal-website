## Mom's Banana Bread

### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient      |
| :------------: | :-------------: |
| 1 Cups         | Sugar           |
| 1/2 Cup        | Butter          |
| 2              | Eggs            |
| 2 Cups         | Flour           |
| 1 Teaspoon     | Baking Soda     |
| 3              | Mashed Bananas  |
| 1/3 Cup        | Chopped Walnuts |

### Method
Cream butter and sugar, and eggs. Add flour, soda, bananas, and walnuts. Pour mixture into 2 medium loaf pans or mom;s preferred method, 3 big greased and floured soup cans 2/3 full. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until golden drown.
