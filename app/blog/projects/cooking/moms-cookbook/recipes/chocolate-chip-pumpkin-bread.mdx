## Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure  | Ingredient                |
| :-------------: | :-----------------------: |
| 3 Cups          | Flour                     |
| 2 Teaspoons     | Baking Powder             |
| 1/2 Teaspoon    | Salt                      |
| 1-1/2 Teaspoon  | Cinnamon                  |
| 1/2 Teaspoon    | Nutmeg                    |
| 1/4 Teaspoon    | Ginger                    |
| 1/4 Teaspoon    | Ground Cloves             |
| 10 Tablespoons  | Unsalted Butter, Softened |
| 2/3 Cup         | Brown Sugar               |
| 2/3 Cup         | Sugar                     |
| 2 Large         | Eggs                      |
| 1 Teaspoon      | Vanilla                   |
| 1x 15 Ounce Can | Pumpkin                   |
| 1 Cup           | Chocolate Chips           |
| 1 Cup           | Chopped Walnuts or Pecans |

### Method
Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Butter and flour a 10 inch bundt pan. Into a bowl, sift the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves. In a bowl with an electric mixer, cream the butter, add the sugar a little at a time and beat the batter until light and fluffy. Beat the eggs a little at a time until combined. Beat in the vanilla and pumpkin until well combined. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Transfer the batter to the prepared pan and smooth top. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until cake tester comes out clean. Let cool in pan on rack for 5 minutes. Invert onto the rack to cool completely. Wrap in plastic and store in refrigerator for up to 5 days. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
