## Sour Cream Coffee Cake

### Ingredients(Main Dish)
| Amount Measure  | Ingredient                 |
| :-------------: | :------------------------: |
| 1 Cup           | Butter at room temperature |
| 3 Cup           | Sugar                      |
| 6               | Eggs at room temperature   |
| 1/4 Teaspoons   | Salt                       |
| 1/4 Teaspoons   | Soda                       |
| 3 Cups          | Flour                      |
| 1 Teaspoons     | Vanilla                    |

### Ingredients(Topping)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient       |
| :------------: | :--------------: |
| 2 Cups         | Chopped Pecans   |
| 8 Tablespoons  | Dark Brown Sugar |
| 4 Teaspoons    | Cinnamon         |

### Method
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Shift salt, soda and flour together 3 times. Alternately add sour cream and flour mixture, ending with flour mixture. Add vanilla, Grease a tube pan. Mix topping ingredients and sprinkle a small amount on the bottom of the pan; add 1/2 of batter. Sprinkle on remaining topping. Bake at 300 degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Do not open oven door for te first hour of baking.
