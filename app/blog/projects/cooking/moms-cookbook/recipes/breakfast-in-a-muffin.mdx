## Breakfast in a Muffin
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure| Ingredient               |
| :-----------: | :----------------------: |
| 12 Ounces     | Bulk Pork Sausage        |
| 3 1/2 Cup     | Baking Mix               |
| 1 Cup         | Shredded Swiss or Chedder|
| 1/2 teaspoon  | Cruched Dried Thyme      |
| 1/2 teaspoon  | Pepper                   |
| 2             | Slightly Beaten Eggs     |
| 1 Cup         | Milk                     |

### Method
Lightly grease twenty-four 2 1/2-inch muffin cups; set aside. In a large skillet cook the sausage till its no longer pink; drain well.\

In a mixing bowl, stir together the sausage, baking mix, cheese, thyme, and pepper. Make a well in the center.\

In a medium mixing bowl, stir together the eggs and milk. Add to dry mixture, stirring until just moistened. Spoon the muffin batter into the 24 prepared muffin cups.\

Bake in a 375 degree oven for 20 minutes or until the muffins are golden brown. Serve warm.\
