## Katie's Hot Artichoke Dip
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure| Ingredient            |
| :-----------: | :-------------------: |
| 1 Can         | Artichoke Hearts      |
| 3/4 Cup       | Mayonnaise            |
| 1/2 Cup       | Grated Parmesan Cheese|

### Method
Drain and chop artichokes (may use potato masher). Add remaining ingredients and pour into a pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes. Serve with crackers, such as Breton brand.\
