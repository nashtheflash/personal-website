## Oyster Snack Crackers
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure| Ingredient          |
| :-----------: | :-----------------: |
| 24 Ounces     | Oyster Crackers     |
| 1 Small Env.  | Ranch Dressing Mix  |
| 1 Tablespoon  | Dried Parsley Flakes|
| 1 Tablespoon  | Driedd Dill Weed    |
| 1 Tablespoon  | Garlic Powder       |
| 1 Cup         | Vegetable Oil       |

### Method
Blend seasonings, envelope and oil well; pour over crackers to coat evenly. Enjoy!

### Additional Information
My Grandma always seemed to have these on hand as I was growing up. Every fall as it gets colder, I get in the mood to make a batch of chili and oyster snack crackers.
