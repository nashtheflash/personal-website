## Baked Eggs and Hash Browns
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure    | Ingredient                  |
| :---------------: | :-------------------------: |
| 10 Ounce          | Frozen Shredded Hash Browns |
| 9 - 10            | Eggs                        |
| 2 Cup             | Shredded Cheddar Cheese     |
| 1/2 Pint          | Whipping Cream              |
| 1/4 Cup           | Onion (Chopped)             |
| 1 to 1 1/2 Pounds | Sausage, Hame, or Bacon     |
| To Taste          | Salt and pepper to Taste    |

### Method
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Spray Bottom and sides of 9 x 13 x 2 inch baking dish with vegetable spray. Line bottom of pan with hash browns. Combine eggs, salt and pepper and pour over hash browns. Sprinkle with half of cheese. Pour cream over layers in pan. Sprinkle with onion, meat and remaining cheese. Can be refrigerated overnight. Nkae about 40 minutes.
