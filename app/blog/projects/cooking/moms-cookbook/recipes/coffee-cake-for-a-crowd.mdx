## Coffee Cake for a Crowd
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient                 |
| :-----------:  | :------------------------: |
| 1 Box          | Yellow or Spice Cake Mix   |
| 3              | Eggs                       |
| 1 Can          | Peach or Apple Pie Filling |
| 3 Tablespoons  | Sugar                      |
| 1 Tablespoon   | Cinnamon                   |

### Method
Combine sugar and cinnamon in small bowl, Mix cake mix, eggs and pi filling for 2 minutes. Place 1/2 of the butter in greased 9 x 13 pan, Sprinkle with 1/2 sugar mixture, top with the rest of the cake mix. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-50 minutes.\
