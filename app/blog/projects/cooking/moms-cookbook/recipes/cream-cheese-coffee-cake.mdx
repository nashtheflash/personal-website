## Cream Cheese Coffee Cake

### Ingredients(Main Dish)
| Amount Measure  | Ingredient    |
| :-------------: | :-----------: |
| 1/4 Cup         | Butter        |
| 8 Ounces        | Cream Cheese  |
| 1 1/2 Cups      | Sugar         |
| 2               | Eggs          |
| 2 Cups          | Flour         |
| 2 Teaspoons     | Baking Powder |
| 1/2 Teaspoons   | Salt          |
| 1 Teaspoons     | Baking Soda   |
| 1/2 Cups        | Milk          |
| 1 Teaspoons     | Vanilla       |

### Ingredients(Topping)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient  |
| :------------: | :---------: |
| 1/4 Cups       | Butter      |
| 1/4 Cups       | Flour       |
| 1 Cups         | Brown Sugar |

### Method
Cream together butter, cream cheese, sugar and eggs. In another bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda. Combine milk vanilla and add to creamed butter mixture, stirring constantly until well blended. Add sifted flour mixture to the creamed ingredients, blending for 3 minutes or until all ingredients are well mixed. Spoon mixture into greased and flouted round cake pans.

Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle over dough mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Can make up to 3 coffee cakes and freeze.
