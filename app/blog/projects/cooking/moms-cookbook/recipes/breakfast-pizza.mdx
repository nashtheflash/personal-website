## Breakfast Pizza
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient                |
| :------------: | :-----------------------: |
| 1 Pound        | Browned Sausage           |
| 1 Package      | Crescent Rolls            |
| 5              | Eggs                      |
| 1/4 Cup        | Milk                      |
| 1/2 Teaspoon   | Salt                      |
| 1 Cup          | Frozen Hash Browns        |
| 1 Cup          | Shredded Cheddar Cheese   |
| 2 Tablepoons   | Shredded Parmeasan Cheese |

### Method
Unroll crescent rolls and press in a 9 x 13 inch pan. Spoon sausage over crust, sprinkle frozen hash browns(thawed), sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over hash browns. Beat together eggs, milk, and salt. Pour mixture over top of pizza. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes and serve.\
