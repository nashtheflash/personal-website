## Todd's Famous Three Cheese Ham Puff
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient                  |
| :------------: | :-------------------------: |
| 6 Large        | Eggs                        |
| 3/4 Cup        | Bisqick                     |
| 1 Cup          | Milk                        |
| 1 Cup          | Cottage Cheese              |
| 8 Ounce        | Monterey Jack Cheese, Cubed |
| 3 Ounce        | Cream Cheese                |
| 1 Pound        | Ham, Cubed, or sliced       |
| 1/4 Teaspoon   | Salt                        |

### Method
Heat oven to 350. Lightly grease 2 quart round casserole or 9 x 13 pan. Beating eggs, add baking mix and milk and beat until smooth. Stir in remaining ingredients; pour into casserole. Bake until knife inserted comes out clean, 50-55 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

### Additional Information
This is one of Todd's favorite breakfast dishes to make for company and one of out favorites to eat.
