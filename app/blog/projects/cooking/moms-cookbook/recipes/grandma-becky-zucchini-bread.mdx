## Grandma Becky's Zucchini Bread
### Ingredients

| Amount Measure | Ingredient       |
| :------------: | :--------------: |
| 3 Large        | Eggs             |
| 1 Cup          | Oil              |
| 2 Cups         | Sugar            |
| 3 Teaspoons    | Vanilla          |
| 3 Cups         | Flour            |
| 1 Teaspoon     | Baking Powder    |
| 3 Teaspoons    | Cinnamon         |
| 1 Teaspoon     | Salt             |
| 1 Teaspoon     | Soda             |
| 2 Cups         | Grated Zucchini  |
| 2 Teaspoons    | Ginger, Optional |
| 1 Cup          | Nuts, Optional   |

### Method
Beat eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla. Then add remaining ingredients. Makes 2 loaves; bake at 350 for 1 hour. Freezes well.
