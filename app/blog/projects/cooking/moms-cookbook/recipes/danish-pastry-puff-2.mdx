## Danish Pastry Puff 2
### Ingredients

#### Step 1 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient |
| :------------: | :--------: |
| 1 Cup          | Flour      |
| 1/2 Cup        | Butter     |
| 2 Tablespoons  | Water      |

#### Step 2 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient     |
| :-----------: | :-------------: |
| 1 Cup          | Water          |
| 1/2 Cup        | Butter         |
| 1 Teaspoon     | Almond Extract |
| 1/3 Cup        | Flour          |
| 3 Large        | Eggs           |

#### Step 3 (Mix Ingredients)
| Amount Measure | Ingredient     |
| :------------: | :------------: |
| 2 Cup          | Powdered Sugar |
| 1/4 Cup        | Butter         |
| 1 Tablespoon   | Vanilla        |

### Method

#### Step 1
Measure flour, cut in butter, sprinkle water and mix with fork. Roll into a ball; divide into 2 parts. Pat dough with hands into 2 strips and place on 12 x 3 increased cookie sheet.
#### Step 2
Mix butter and water, bring to boil. Add almond; remove from heat. Stir in flour immediately and stir constantly to keep from lumping. When it is smooth. Divide mixture between 2 dough strips, spread each to cover. Bake 55-60 minutes at 350. It will be puffy. Remove from oven, cool slightly.

#### Step 3
Mix ingredients to make frosting, spread each puff. Sprinkle with nuts if desired.

### Additional Information
This was a recipe given to me at my wedding shower, and one I have seen many ties in good old church cookbooks. It is a favorite of family and friends.
